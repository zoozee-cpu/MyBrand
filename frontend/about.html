About ZKS Fragrances

"Built from passion. Bottled with precision."

The Beginning

A Scent for Every Story

ZKS Fragrances began with a simple frustration: exceptional fragrances existed everywhere in the world — except here, at a price that felt fair for the Pakistani consumer. We believed that luxury scent was a right, not a privilege reserved for those who could afford imported labels at three times the value.

What started as a personal obsession with scent composition evolved into something bigger — a collection of eight meticulously crafted compositions, each designed to tell a story and to become the signature of whoever chooses to wear it.

Every fragrance in the ZKS collection was developed through months of iteration — testing note combinations, refining pyramid structures, and testing longevity on different skin types across Pakistan's climate spectrum. The result is eight compositions that work beautifully whether you're navigating Karachi's humidity or Islamabad's crisp winter air.

🌿

Premium Ingredients

Artisan Composition

Sourcing Philosophy

What Goes Into Every Bottle

🌸

Floral Accords

Rose absolute, jasmine sambac, peony, and lily of the valley — sourced for their natural depth, not synthetic approximations.

🪵

Wood & Resins

Oud, cedarwood, sandalwood, and patchouli selected for longevity and depth — the foundation that makes a fragrance last hours on skin.

🍊

Citrus & Spice

Bergamot, yuzu, saffron, and cardamom — carefully calibrated to open each fragrance with energy without overwhelming what follows.

A Note from the Founder

Why I Started ZKS

"I've always believed that the way you smell is one of the most intimate expressions of who you are. It's not about fashion — it's about the invisible trail you leave in every room, the memory you trigger in someone years later, the way a single spray can shift your entire mood."

"I started ZKS because I couldn't find fragrances in Pakistan that felt genuinely luxurious without costing a fortune — so I made them myself. Each of our eight scents is something I would personally wear, and something I think the people I care about deserve to experience."

"This is not a side project. This is a mission. And it's only just beginning."

Muhammad Zakria

Founder, ZKS Fragrances
