Return & Exchange Policy

All sales are final on opened bottles. Due to hygiene regulations and international HAZMAT shipping restrictions governing the transport of alcoholic fragrance compounds, opened perfume bottles cannot be returned or exchanged under any circumstance.

We understand that scent is deeply personal, which is why we encourage you to reach out via WhatsApp before purchasing if you have any questions about a fragrance — our team is happy to help you find the right match.

Unopened Bottle Exchange:

  • Unopened bottles may be exchanged within 48 hours of delivery, with original packaging intact and photographic proof of the unopened condition.
  • To initiate an exchange, WhatsApp us at +92 340 7122385 with your Order ID and clear photos of the product and its packaging.
  • Exchange shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the product was damaged or incorrect upon arrival.
  • Exchanges are processed within 3–5 business days of receiving the returned product.

We reserve the right to refuse an exchange if the product shows signs of having been opened, tampered with, or returned without proper packaging.

Shipping Policy

ZKS Fragrances ships across Pakistan. Here's what you need to know:

  • Estimated Delivery: 3–7 business days across Pakistan.
  • Major Cities (Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad): 2–4 business days estimated.
  • Shipping Fees: Quoted individually via WhatsApp based on your delivery location. We aim to keep this as fair as possible.
  • Order Tracking: Tracking information will be shared via WhatsApp once your order has been dispatched.
  • Packaging: All orders are securely packaged to prevent damage during transit.
  • International Shipping: We do not currently ship internationally due to HAZMAT regulations governing the air transport of fragrances (see HAZMAT Notice below).

⚠️ Important: Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary during public holidays, extreme weather conditions, or high-demand periods. We will keep you informed via WhatsApp of any delays.

Privacy Policy

ZKS Fragrances is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect and use your data.

What We Collect

  • Your full name and delivery address (provided voluntarily during the WhatsApp checkout process).
  • Your WhatsApp phone number (used for order communication).
  • Basic order data including items purchased, quantities, and total amount.

How We Use Your Data

  • To process and fulfill your order.
  • To communicate with you about your order status via WhatsApp.
  • To maintain an internal order record for fulfillment and customer service purposes.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third-party marketing companies.
  • We do not store payment information — all payments are handled directly between the customer and our payment method of choice.
  • We do not send unsolicited marketing messages without your consent.

Data Retention: Order data is stored securely for fulfillment purposes and standard business record-keeping. If you wish to have your data removed from our records, please contact us via WhatsApp.

Contact: For any privacy-related questions, reach us at +92 340 7122385 via WhatsApp.

HAZMAT Notice

⚠️ Fragrances are classified as flammable liquids for air transport purposes under international shipping regulations (IATA DGR — Dangerous Goods Regulations).

This classification affects how ZKS Fragrances can be shipped:

  • Domestic Shipping (Pakistan): All shipments comply with Pakistani postal and courier regulations for the domestic transport of fragrances. We work with carriers who handle fragrance products responsibly.
  • International Shipping: We are currently unable to ship internationally. The HAZMAT classification of alcoholic fragrance compounds restricts their carriage on commercial passenger aircraft, and the specialized freight services required for compliant international fragrance shipping are not currently available to us at a price that allows for fair consumer pricing.
  • Safe Handling: All ZKS fragrances are packaged in compliance with domestic transport regulations, with appropriate cushioning and sealed packaging to prevent breakage or leakage.

We are actively working to expand our shipping capabilities. Updates will be announced on our Instagram (@muhammadzakria917) and communicated to existing customers.
